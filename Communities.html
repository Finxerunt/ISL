Communities

Our local communities, environments, and contemporaries serve as the basis for our foundation and growth as individuals. Proper education facilities and programs are essential for fostering well-informed populations and their ability to produce a stable source of revenue for their respective communities. Proper health facilities are crucial in the assurance of healthy individuals and their ability to bring about valuable outlooks and changes for the community. Physical facilities and programs (volunteer, youth activism, career development, network), are necessary in the upbringing of productive and successful individuals and their ability to foster a fertile and fruitful environment for their community. However, a number of modern communities lack the proper facilities to encourage healthy growth and stability and far too little efforts are being made to change this debilitation.

The goal of the ISL is to discover where and when such discrepancies occur. We also examine why these various discrepancies occur in the first place and how we can go about tackling and resolving these issues on a local level. The research we produce goes towards creating films, various journal articles, and press releases that help bring attention to the problem in a local community. The research conducted by the ISL for local communities goes on to assist the Finxerunt Policy Action Institute to take action. Once they are able to successfully tackle the problem, we are able to ensure that these local issues are resolved, leading all residents from this community to have a brighter tomorrow.
