Our mission

Across the Spectrum of Socioeconomics is a research journal published by the International Socioeconomics Laboratory that aims to bridge the prevalent issues between economics and social science. Students from around the world come together to conduct extensive research and produce quality non-partisan conclusions. It is a free and open access journal that has socioeconomic research of the highest caliber which goes towards creating a brighter and more sustainable future for all. Through the lobbying initiatives taken by the Finxerunt Policy Action Institute, the research allows students to take their findings and create policy and legislation.
